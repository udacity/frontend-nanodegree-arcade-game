Here's how to play:

  1. Choose your character:
    • Press b for the bold boy.
    • Press c for the cunning cat.
    • Press p for the powerful princess.
  2. Press the arrow keys to get your petite character across the stony path without running into any pesky bugs.
  3. Collect the splendid stars along the way for more points.

1 Trip Across = 5 points. 1 Star = 2 points. 1 Bug Bump = -1 point.

Good luck!
